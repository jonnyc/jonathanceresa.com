About Jonathan

My name is Jonathan Ceresa and I am a seasoned Web Developer, Wall Street veteran and entrepreneur. I received my Bachelors degree in Computer Science with a concentration in finance from Syracuse University in New York. I began my career in finance, then moved to web development, then management and ultimately launched my private consulting firm Tech Argonauts.

I have extensive experience communicating with web designers, project managers, developers, implementation teams, investors and multiple other stakeholders to fully deliver on product expectations, deadlines and cost commitments. My entrepreneurial experience has allowed me to develop a wide variety of skills and assume a diverse array of professional responsibilities. I have also taken it upon myself to learn and excel at new technologies for my own development. I understand the full life cycle of a Web Application project.

I am a very outgoing, hard working and detail oriented person. Owing to the fact that I am a born team player, it is easy for me to work well in a team fostered environment. I am confident given my previous employment experience and training that I will succeed in any business technology role.
